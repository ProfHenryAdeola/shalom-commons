Our Vision

The Shalom Initiative (TSI) is a global movement dedicated to advancing human flourishing through scientific discovery, transformative education, compassionate service, ethical leadership, and worshipful living.

Our Pillars

Shalom Health

Healthy ageing, precision medicine, oral-systemic health, cancer, dementia, AI and multi-omics.

Shalom Learning

Leadership, mentorship, scientific training and education.

Shalom Worship

Creativity, worship, faith and science dialogue.

Shalom Impact

Community transformation, scholarships and global partnerships.

Founder's Vision

"We aspire to leave every person, every partnership, every community, and every generation better than we found them."
